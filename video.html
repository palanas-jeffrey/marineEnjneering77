Tour in the Engine Room

Engine room is one of the major part of the ship. It contains the neccessary machineries to power up, proppel, steer, control and other things which describes it as the heart of the ship. Engaging with the said field is not an easy task. One needs to be prepared to work even in the extreme conditions and be equiped with the neccessary knowledge to stay safe.

The main engine gives the power to drive the proppeller of the ship. Auxiliary engine drives the alternator to generate electrical power. Various auxiliary systems such as boiler, air compressor, purifier, cooling, lubricating, fuel and others completes the structure of the engine room. Courtesy to youtube.com.

Main Engine

The main engine of ship is a two-stroke diesel engine. Most of it is made from Man B&W Engine Maker. Due to the advancement of technology, electronic controlled engine or Intelligent Engine is well known in the industry. Intelligent Engine has a central electronic control system which acts as the brain of the system and which monitors and evaluate the general condition to keep the operating parameter within limits and maintain the performance of the engine at the higher side.

The control system may vary but the basic principles stays the same. Courtesy to youtube.com.
